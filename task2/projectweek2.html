Chicken Biryani

DESCRIPTION

One of the most royal delicacies that you can enjoy on any occasion or festival, Chicken Biryani is the epitome of a one-pot meal. Well, no one can resist the sight of the aromatic and delicious Chicken Biryani recipe. If you are also craving that, then you need not go anywhere as we have got this super-easy Biryani recipe for you. So, what are you waiting for? Do try this delicious Chicken Biryani recipe and enjoy it with your loved ones.

    Instructions

    Cook the Rice

    1. In a pot, combine the soaked rice, water, salt, ghee, and spices.
    2. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low and simmer, covered, for 15-20 minutes or until the rice is cooked.
    3. Drain the rice and keep it aside.

    Marinate the Chicken

    1. In a bowl, combine the chicken, yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric powder, garam masala powder, salt, and lemon juice.
    2. Marinate the chicken for at least 30 minutes, or preferably overnight.

    Cook the Biryani

    1. Heat ghee in a large pot or Dutch oven.
    2. Add the sliced onion and green chili, and fry until golden brown.
    3. Add the ginger-garlic paste and fry for a few more seconds.
    4. Add the marinated chicken and cook until it changes color.
    5. Add the red chili powder, turmeric powder, garam masala powder, and salt.
    6. Cook for a few more minutes, stirring occasionally.
    7. Add the cooked rice, coriander leaves, mint leaves, and fried onions.
    8. Mix everything gently.
    9. Cover the pot with a tight-fitting lid and cook on low heat for 10-15 minutes, or until the flavors are well-combined.

    Serve Hot

    Serve the Chicken Biryani hot, garnished with additional coriander leaves and fried onions. Enjoy with raita or yogurt.

    Tips

    • For a richer flavor, you can add a few drops of kewra water to the rice.
    • You can also add a few strands of saffron soaked in warm milk to the rice for a beautiful color and aroma.
    • To make the biryani even more flavorful, you can cook it in a clay pot (handi).

    Enjoy your homemade Chicken Biryani!
